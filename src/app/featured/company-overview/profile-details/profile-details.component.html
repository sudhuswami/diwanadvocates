Dr. Farrukh Khan

Dr. Farrukh is the administrative head and a partner of the firm. He is an enthusiastic and creative professional with excellent organizational and communication skills. His practical hands-on-approach invariably leads to achieving the best possible result in a given situation.

Dr. Farrukh has diversified litigation exposure. His litigation skills encompasses from criminal to civil and corporate practice under Indian legal framework. He has represented several leading Banks and Financial Institutions, Government Entities, Real Estate Consortiums, Project Finance Companies, Not for Profit entities etc. in assortment of litigations including of civil and criminal nature.

Dr. Farrukh is particularly experienced in litigation and arbitration, including white collar crime. He is a highly respected specialist in procedural and arbitration law business issues. He has considerable experience in corporate law and advising international and domestic companies. Dr Farrukh advises General Counsel, CEOs, and corporate boards on matters involving litigation, corporate governance and ethics. In the boardroom and the courtroom, his philosophy of early evaluation of risks and corporate exposure consistently produces cost-effective resolution of disputes.

On non-contentious side Dr Farrukh has a particular focus on the property aspects of infrastructure developments for government and semi-government authorities including advising on issues associated with valuation and resumption matters, easements, compulsory acquisition, native title/aboriginal land rights and landowner negotiations and disputes. His areas of expertise are: construction and engineering (major projects and infrastructure, PPP projects and dispute resolution) and documentation (project deeds/development agreements, consultancy agreements, novation agreements, managing contractor agreements, alliance agreements, lump sum building contracts, design and construct contracts, supply agreements, subcontract agreements, finance tripartite deeds, and construction direct agreements).

He also has worked in all aspects of IPR related areas,Dr. Farrukh comfortably devisesintegrated packages in all the areas of IPR includingpatent, copyright, trademark, sports IP, licensing, due diligence, etc. He also has expertise on the issues relevant to deal with ambush marketing, management of copyrights for event and marketing campaign. He is a regular speaker on IPR related forums and conferences and member of several IPR international organizations. Judgments in many cases where he has appearedhave been reported by several prestigious law journals of India.

During his stint with law-firms abroad, Dr. Farrukh got opportunity to understand nuances of business and industry. Thus, armed with this knowledge and experience, Dr. Farrukh is perfectly at ease in advising and devising legal documentation covering all aspects of commercial transactions including investments plans, drafting and processing documentations in relation to merger, demerger collaboration, joint ventures etc.He has also negotiated and structured domestic and overseas transactions pertaining to Oil Gas & Power Sectors.

Before joiningDiwan Advocates, Dr. Farrukh spent number of years as a corporate attorney in several Middle-East countries. He has also worked for a Spanish corporateconglomerate as itsLegal Advisor. He was also associated with United Nations as a Legal Advisor to Regional Director (Middle East-North Africa Region) of IIMSAM.

Dr. Farrukh is a frequent speaker on law-related matters at various forums. He also has passion to interact with law students and, he has been speakers at some prestigious law Schools in India.
